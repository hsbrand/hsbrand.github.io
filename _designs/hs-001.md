Buy In is for players who are ready to work, support their teammates, and put the goals of the group ahead of their own. It tells anyone watching that you are committed to the team and ready to be part of something bigger than yourself.

### The Story

HS-001 was our first design, and it felt like the right place to begin.

Every hockey team is looking for players who buy in—players who believe in the style of play, commit to the team’s goals, and support the direction of the group. We liked it as a hockey message, but also as a motto for Hockey Stock itself.
