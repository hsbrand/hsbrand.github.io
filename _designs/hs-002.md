A dangle is what happens when a player puts the puck on a string and dances around an opponent. It is skill, confidence, creativity, and just enough swagger to make everyone on the bench yell, “Dangles!”

### The Story

HS-002 was a fun one that we created together. It began as a messy drawing: the word “Dangle” followed by the long, winding path of a player carrying the puck through traffic.

We cleaned it up and turned it into something more visually polished, but kept the movement and puck-on-a-string feeling that inspired it.
